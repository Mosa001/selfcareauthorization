About Us

We are Sun Power Energy Company, an energy company currently executing a solar energy project in the US.

Want to Know About Us?

We are energy company currently executing a solar energy project in the US. This project is expected to last for 18 months. As we hope that you will be an asset to us by working as a Personal Assistance to our manager, I wish to state that this job could only offer $600 weekly at this time and $2,400 monthly.

We usually increase the pay after three months. There is also a possibility of making you a permanent staff of the company after this project.
Your dedication and evaluation by your manager will be a major determinant on making the decision thereafter.


An average of 40 hours is requiring weekly but with a flexibility of responding to any urgency at any time, through email, phone call or text message.

JOB DESCRIPTION:

Please see below itemized list of what are expected from you. This is also your job descriptions.

PROJECTS COORDINATOR:

Kindly note that you are not a project manager, but your job entails that you gather information about progress on projects from project manager and document every data collected. You will also be liasing with the accounts since you will be purchasing some minor items for projects if needed. Some of the deliverables are listed below.

- Weekly/bi-weekly meetings with Project managers to review progress of project
- Flexible to work accurately at your convenient time
- High speed Internet connection and capable home computer
- Able to multi-task on a regular basis
- Independent worker who does not need supervision
- Extremely reliable
- Able to learn new procedures quickly
- Take incoming project calls and set appointments for project managers

PROCUREMENT:

You are also responsible for the purchase of minor tools, first aid kits, electronic gadgets, minor solar energy components and sometimes pay external third-party contractors during project execution.
Sometimes we rely on third party to execute some tasks, you will be responsible for their logistics ,payments and documentation.
You must approve the best products that have low prices after reviewing and analyzing all options. Please note that quality is a factor in procurement.

DATA HANDLING:

The task of maintaining accurate records of purchases and project achievements falls squarely on your shoulders.
This entails managing the inventories of the products.
You are to ensure that the department has important details, such as the suppliers’ qualifications, delivery times, potential development and products.

The project Management Lead will always let you know if they need anything on site through emails and calls and he will copy the account department as well so that the process could be expedited.
The accounts department will make payment to you via direct deposit to your account or write check in your name whenever is there urgent need for any procurement or payment.
Please take your time to read and understand your role and let me know if you need to any clarification. However, this job is a flexible job that can be done in your free time without disturbing your other jobs.
